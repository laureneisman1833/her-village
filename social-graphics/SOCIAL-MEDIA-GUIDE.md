# Her Village — Social Media Graphics Pack

> Designed to drive traffic to https://her-village.org
> Brand keywords: single mothers support, single mom community, resources for single mothers, financial help for single moms

---

## 1. Feed Posts (1080×1080px) — 4 Designs

| File | Theme | Best Used For |
|------|-------|---------------|
| `feed/find-your-village.png` | Welcome/community invite | First post to introduce the brand |
| `feed/youre-not-alone.png` | Supportive message | Emotional connection, shares |
| `feed/resources-for-strong-moms.png` | Content preview card | Resource/benefits highlight |
| `feed/join-our-community.png` | CTA signup card | Direct response / signup drive |

**Posting Strategy**: Post 3x/week. Cycle through: emotional (You're Not Alone) → resource (Resources for Strong Moms) → CTA (Join Our Community). Use "Find Your Village" as the main pinned post.

**Suggested captions**: Include 3–5 relevant hashtags from: #singlemom #singlemother #singlemomsupport #singlemomlife #hervillage #singlemomstrong #momcommunity

## Design System Summary

| Element | Color | Hex |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Headings | Deep Navy | `#1a1a2e` |
| Accent/Buttons | Terracotta | `#c67a5c` |
| Backgrounds | Warm Cream | `#f8f4f0` |
| Secondary BG | Soft Tan | `#e8d5c4` |
| Body Text | Medium Gray | `#555555` |
| CTA Dark BG | Navy Gradient | `#1a1a2e → #2d2d5e` |

- **Fonts**: Nunito (headings), Poppins (body), Inter (captions)
- **Vibe**: Warm, supportive, community-focused, cloud-like softness
- **Keywords**: financial help, community, support, grants, single mom, single mother, parenting, resources

## Traffic Strategy Alignment

These graphics are designed to support the SEO & content strategy from `/home/team/shared/her-village-traffic-strategy.md`:

1. **Instagram** → Drive to blog posts (financial help, support groups, etc.) → email signup
2. **Pinterest** → Long-tail traffic engine for blog content (evergreen shelf life)
3. **Facebook/LinkedIn** → Professional credibility + community-building
4. **Stories** → Daily engagement, swipe-up to key content
